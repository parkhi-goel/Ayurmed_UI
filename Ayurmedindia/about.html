WHO ARE WE?

Ayurmed is a nutraceutical brand brought forth by Sidhbali Ayush Pvt. Ltd. We, at Ayurmed, intend to creatively address the gaps in the nutrition sector. This we would be able to achieve by developing easy avenues of consuming nutritional components.

DROP, DISSOLVE, DRINK


This is how easy taking care of your health is. On-the-go Nutrition is here to stay. We, at Ayurmed, have come up with our own effervescent tablets. You just need to drop one tablet at a time in water. That’s it. Ayurmed’s Effervescents currently address the following paradigms: Iron, Calcium, Digestion and Multi-Vitamins.

Our company

Our Experience of nutritions is defining the health.

Nutraceutical came to life because what was once a fragmented health and wellness marketplace demanded more thought and purpose, especially if people were going to have what they needed to take charge of their health.

Our founding brands, Kal and Thompson, were the first nutritional supplements in the United States, setting the stage for Nutraceutical to cultivate a portfolio of brands that stands for superior quality in the health and wellness industry.
